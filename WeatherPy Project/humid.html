Latitude vs. Humidity

Statistical analysis:

  • The value of r-squared was 0.0033060312335673613.
  • The line equation for linear regression was: y = 0.03x + 71.53..

There does not appear to be much correlation between latitude and humidity. This visual observation is supported by the low r-squared value. However we can tell humidity mostly stays above 50% regardless of latitude. The vertical spread (between 100% and 20%) also remains fairly consistent regardless of latitude. The outliers (humidity below 20%) are concentrated roughly between 10 and 45 degrees latitude.
