Latitude vs. Wind Speed

Statistical analysis:

  • The value of r-squared was 0.05095351063895061.
  • The line equation for linear regression was: y = 0.04x + 8.17.

The relationship between wind speed and latitude seems to be the inverse of the relationship between humidity and latitude. Here, wind speed is concentrated on the lower half of the graph, mostly below 15 miles per hour regardless of latitude. The outliers (above 25mph) are concentrated between 20 and 80 degrees lattitude.
